## Quick reference
- **Brand:** Chennu Naga Venkata Sai (a.k.a. Venkat) — AI/ML Engineer & CS Researcher
- **Positioning:** Scholarly + production — 5 publications, 50+ citations, NVIDIA/AWS/Anthropic certified
- **Key files:**
